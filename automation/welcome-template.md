:wave: Hey {{first_name}}, welcome to #zb-dx!

This is the shared knowledge base for developers building on the ZeroBias platform — `zerobias-sdk`, `zerobias-client`, and `zerobias-angular-client`.

The repo is public and self-documenting: https://github.com/zerobias-org/zb-dx

Easiest way to start: **fork it, clone your fork, and point your LLM at it.** The repo has `CLAUDE.md` and `AGENTS.md` at the root — your AI assistant will pick those up automatically and walk you through everything: how to register your participant profile, how to capture friction, where the patterns live, what's worth building.

When you start using the SDK in your own app, you'll need an `.npmrc` for the private `@zerobias-org/*` packages — ping me when you get there.

Questions, friction, ideas? Post in #zb-dx — that's what it's for.
